## One CRM at a time

Each organization can have **one active CRM integration** at a time (Salesforce, HubSpot, or Zoho). You can disconnect and reconnect a different CRM at any time.

---

## Where to manage integrations

Go to **Settings → Integrations** in the dashboard.

Each integration card shows its connection status and a **Connect** or **Disconnect** button.

---

## Security

All CRM access tokens and refresh tokens are **encrypted at rest** using AES-256-GCM. Tokens are never exposed in the UI.

The connect flow is protected against OAuth CSRF attacks using a one-time nonce cookie. The callback verifies the nonce before processing any tokens.
