## What HubSpot enables

When connected, your agents can:
- Create and update Contacts, Companies, and Deals
- Query HubSpot records to personalize conversations
- Send data into your HubSpot pipelines automatically

---

## Connecting HubSpot

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Integrations">
    In the sidebar, click **Settings**, then **Integrations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect HubSpot">
    Click the **Connect HubSpot** button on the HubSpot card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize in HubSpot">
    You'll be redirected to HubSpot's OAuth authorization page. Sign in with a HubSpot user that has the required permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your portal">
    If you have multiple HubSpot portals, select the correct one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant permissions">
    Click **Connect app** to authorize Duvefy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to Duvefy">
    You'll be redirected back to the Integrations page with HubSpot showing as connected.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What topics can do with HubSpot

| Operation | Topic instruction example |
|-----------|--------------------------|
| Create a Contact | "Create a new Contact in HubSpot with the visitor's name and email." |
| Create a Deal | "Create a new Deal in HubSpot linked to the visitor's contact." |
| Update a Contact | "Update the Contact's lifecycle stage to 'Marketing Qualified Lead'." |
| Query records | "Search HubSpot for a Contact with this email address." |

## Disconnecting HubSpot

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Integrations">
    Click **Settings** → **Integrations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Disconnect">
    Click **Disconnect** on the HubSpot card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    A red confirmation panel appears inline. Click **Yes, disconnect** to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Disconnecting removes stored tokens. Any topics relying on HubSpot will fail until you reconnect.
</Warning>
