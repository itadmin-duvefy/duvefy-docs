## What Zoho CRM enables

When connected, your agents can:
- Create and update Leads, Contacts, Deals, and other Zoho modules
- Query Zoho records to personalize conversations

---

## Connecting Zoho CRM

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Integrations">
    In the sidebar, click **Settings**, then **Integrations**.

    <Info>
      📸 **Screenshot needed** — `images/zoho-connect-step1.png`

      The Integrations page with the Zoho CRM card visible and the **Connect Zoho** button highlighted.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect Zoho">
    Click the **Connect Zoho** button on the Zoho CRM card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize in Zoho">
    You'll be redirected to Zoho's OAuth login page. Sign in with a Zoho CRM user that has API access.

    <Info>
      📸 **Screenshot needed** — `images/zoho-connect-step3.png`

      The Zoho OAuth authorization page showing the permission grant screen with the Duvefy app name and requested CRM scopes listed.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant permissions">
    Review and accept the requested permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to Duvefy">
    You'll be redirected back to the Integrations page with Zoho showing as connected.

    <Info>
      📸 **Screenshot needed** — `images/zoho-connect-step5.png`

      The Integrations page with the Zoho CRM card showing a green "Connected" status badge and the **Disconnect** button.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What topics can do with Zoho CRM

| Operation | Topic instruction example |
|-----------|--------------------------|
| Create a Lead | "Create a new Lead in Zoho with the visitor's name and email." |
| Update a Contact | "Update the Contact's status in Zoho." |
| Query records | "Search Zoho for a Lead matching the visitor's email." |

## Disconnecting Zoho CRM

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Integrations">
    Click **Settings** → **Integrations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Disconnect">
    Click **Disconnect** on the Zoho CRM card.

    <Info>
      📸 **Screenshot needed** — `images/zoho-disconnect.png`

      The Zoho CRM card in connected state showing the inline red confirmation panel with **Yes, disconnect** and **Cancel** buttons.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    A red confirmation panel appears inline. Click **Yes, disconnect** to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Disconnecting removes stored tokens. Any topics relying on Zoho will fail until you reconnect.
</Warning>
