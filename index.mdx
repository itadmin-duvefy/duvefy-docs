## What is Duvefy?

Duvefy enables organizations to build and deploy AI chat agents that can engage website visitors, collect information, trigger CRM workflows, and process payments — all configured in plain English, no coding required.

## How the platform works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Your organization is created">
    Duvefy sets up your organization, assigns a subscription plan, and sends an invitation to your owner email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You build an agent">
    Give your agent a name, mission, and knowledge base. Customize the widget colors and launcher icon.

    <Info>
      📸 **Screenshot needed** — `images/intro-agent-edit.png`

      Agent edit page showing the Basic Info tab with Name, Mission, and agent type fields filled in.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="You define topics">
    Write plain-English workflows (topics) that the agent can trigger — from collecting a donation to creating a CRM record.

    <Info>
      📸 **Screenshot needed** — `images/intro-topics.png`

      Topics tab inside an agent showing a list of topics with their labels, enabled toggles, and the instructions text area open for one topic.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="You embed the widget">
    Copy a single `<script>` tag into your website. The agent is live instantly.

    <Info>
      📸 **Screenshot needed** — `images/intro-embed.png`

      Embed tab showing the generated script snippet with the Copy button highlighted.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Visitors interact">
    Your visitors chat with the agent. It executes workflows, syncs with your CRM, and processes payments — automatically.

    <Info>
      📸 **Screenshot needed** — `images/intro-widget-live.png`

      The live chat widget open on a website, showing a sample conversation with the agent.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Platform access

<Note>
  Duvefy is **invitation-only**. Organizations are created and managed by Duvefy's internal operations team. If you're expecting access, check your email for an invitation link.
</Note>

| Role | Access level |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full control over agents, settings, team, and integrations |
| **Admin** | Create/edit agents and topics, manage integrations and payments |
| **Member** | View conversations, leads, and analytics (read-only) |
