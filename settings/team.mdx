## Overview

Manage your team at **Settings → Team**. Here you can see all current members, invite new ones, change roles, and remove people who no longer need access.

---

## Inviting a team member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Team">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar, then **Team**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite Member">
    Click the **Invite Member** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter their email and role">
    Enter the team member's email address and select a role: **Admin** or **Member**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invitation">
    Click **Send Invite**. An email is sent with a one-time link to set their password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When the invited person clicks the link:
1. They are taken to `/accept-invite`
2. They set a password
3. They are immediately added to the organization and can sign in

---

## Who can invite whom

| Your role | Can invite |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Owner** | Admins and Members |
| **Admin** | Members only |
| **Member** | Cannot invite |

---

## Changing a member's role

Only owners can change roles.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the member">
    In the team list, locate the member whose role you want to change.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change the role">
    Click the role dropdown next to their name and select the new role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm the change. It takes effect immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Removing a team member

Owners and admins can remove members. Owners can also remove admins.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the member">
    Locate the member in the team list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Remove">
    Click the **Remove** button next to their name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm the removal. The user loses access immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Removing a team member does not delete their Duvefy account — it only removes them from your organization.
</Note>

---

## Resending an invitation

If a team member hasn't received or has lost their invite email, you can request a resend. Contact [support@duvefy.com](mailto:support@duvefy.com) if you need assistance resending invitations.

---

## Security note

Every invitation, role change, and removal is recorded in the **audit log** (Settings → Audit Logs), giving you a complete history of team access changes.
