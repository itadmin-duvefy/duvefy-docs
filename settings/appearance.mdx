## Theme toggle

Duvefy supports **Light** (default) and **Dark** mode for the dashboard interface.

You can toggle the theme in two places:
- **Sidebar** — Click the moon/sun icon at the bottom of the left sidebar
- **Settings → Appearance** — Use the toggle on this settings page

Your preference is saved locally in your browser and persists across sessions.

---

## Light mode

The default theme with white backgrounds and blue accents — clean and professional for brightly lit environments.

---

## Dark mode

Dark backgrounds (`#0F1629` page, `#1A2540` cards) with lighter text — easier on the eyes in low-light environments.

---

## Per-user preference

Theme preference is stored per-browser, not per-account. If you sign in on a different browser or device, it will default to light mode until you toggle it again.
