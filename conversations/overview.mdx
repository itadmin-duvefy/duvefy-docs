## Overview

Every time a visitor interacts with an agent, a conversation is created. Duvefy takes a **privacy-first approach** to conversation storage: transcripts are never permanently stored in Duvefy's database. Instead, when a conversation ends, the transcript is synced to your connected CRM — then deleted from Duvefy's systems.

---

## How it works

1. **During the conversation** — messages are held temporarily to provide context to the AI agent.
2. **When the conversation ends** — the transcript is assembled and synced to your CRM as a Note on the relevant record.
3. **After sync** — messages are permanently deleted from Duvefy's database.

If the sync fails (e.g., your CRM token expired), the transcript is encrypted and queued for up to 48 hours while automatic retries run. After 48 hours, the transcript is discarded.

---

## Where transcripts are stored in your CRM

| CRM | Object |
|---|---|
| **Salesforce** | Note linked to the identified record (Lead, Contact, Opportunity, etc.) |
| **HubSpot** | Note engagement linked to the identified record (Contact, Deal, Ticket, etc.) |
| **Zoho** | Note linked to the identified module record (Leads, Contacts, Deals, etc.) |

The transcript is attached to whichever CRM record was created or looked up during the conversation. If the agent created a Lead, the note lands on that Lead. If it only queried a Contact, the note lands on that Contact.

---

## Anonymous conversations

An anonymous conversation is one where no CRM record was identified — for example, an information-only agent that never interacted with your CRM.

You can configure what happens to these transcripts **per agent** in Agent Settings → Overview:

| Setting | Behavior |
|---|---|
| **Discard** (default) | Transcript is not stored anywhere — deleted from Duvefy when the conversation ends. |
| **Link to catch-all CRM record** | Transcript is attached to a designated CRM record of your choice. |

To use the catch-all option, you'll need to provide:
- **CRM object type** — the type of record to link to (e.g., `Contact`, `Account`)
- **CRM record ID** — the specific record in your CRM that will collect these transcripts

<Note>
  Anonymous behavior is only configurable when a CRM integration is connected to your organization.
</Note>

---

## No CRM connected

If your organization has no CRM connected, conversation transcripts are **not stored anywhere**. Messages are discarded when the conversation ends.

This is the most privacy-preserving configuration. If you want transcripts to be stored, connect a CRM in **Settings → Integrations**.

---

## Retry and data expiry

If the CRM sync fails (e.g., your OAuth token expired), Duvefy will:
1. Encrypt the transcript and queue it for retry
2. Automatically retry every 30 minutes for up to 48 hours
3. Discard the transcript after 48 hours regardless of sync status

To prevent failed syncs from accumulating, ensure your CRM integration stays connected and tokens are kept valid.

---

## Engaged conversations

A conversation is considered **engaged** when at least one topic action completed successfully — for example, a CRM record was created, a payment was initiated, or an email was sent.

Engaged conversation counts are shown on the analytics dashboard.

---

## Access by role

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| **Owner** | View all conversations |
| **Admin** | View all conversations |
| **Member** | View all conversations (read-only) |

<Note>
  Because transcripts are synced to your CRM and deleted from Duvefy, the Conversations dashboard view will not show full message history for completed conversations. Full transcripts are available in your CRM.
</Note>
