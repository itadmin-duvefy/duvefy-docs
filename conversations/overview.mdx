## Overview

Every time a visitor sends a message to an agent, a conversation is created. The full message history is stored and viewable in the dashboard by all team members.

Access conversations from **Conversations** in the left sidebar.

---

## Conversation list

The conversations list shows all interactions across all your agents, sorted by most recent. For each conversation you can see:
- The agent the visitor was talking to
- The date and time the conversation started
- A preview of the first message

Click any conversation to view the full message history.

---

## Conversation detail

Inside a conversation you can see:
- The full back-and-forth message history between the visitor and the agent
- Which topics were triggered (if any)
- Data collected from the visitor (e.g., name, email, amount)

---

## Engaged conversations

A conversation is considered **engaged** when at least one topic action completed successfully — for example, a CRM record was created, a payment was initiated, or an email was sent.

Engaged conversation counts are shown on the analytics dashboard.

---

## CRM conversations

When a CRM is connected, conversations that involved CRM activity are also visible under **CRM → Conversations** in the sidebar. This view filters for conversations where the agent interacted with your CRM.

---

## Access by role

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| **Owner** | View all conversations |
| **Admin** | View all conversations |
| **Member** | View all conversations (read-only) |

All roles can view conversations. No role can delete or modify conversation history from the dashboard.

---

## Data retention

Conversation history is stored indefinitely. There is currently no automatic purge or expiry.
