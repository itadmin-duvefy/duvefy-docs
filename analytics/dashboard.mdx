## Overview

The dashboard is your organization's analytics hub. It gives you a real-time view of how your agents are performing — from message volumes to CRM record creation to payments processed.

Access it from **Overview** in the left sidebar.

---

## Period filter

All metrics (except "Messages This Month") respect the period filter at the top of the page:

| Option | What it covers |
|--------|---------------|
| **This Month** | Current calendar month to date |
| **Last 90 Days** | Rolling 90-day window |
| **Last 12 Months** | Rolling 12-month window |
| **All Time** | Since the organization was created |

---

## KPI cards

### Messages This Month
Current month's message count versus your plan limit. Always shows the current billing period regardless of the period filter.

A progress bar fills as you approach the limit. At **80% usage**, an upgrade prompt appears. At **100%**, the agent stops responding until the next period.

### Conversations
Total number of conversations started in the selected period, with a sub-count of **engaged conversations** — those where at least one action was successfully executed.

Clicking the card takes you to the Conversations list.

### Engagement Rate
Percentage of conversations that resulted in at least one successful action. Calculated as:

```
engaged conversations / total conversations × 100%
```

A higher engagement rate means more visitors are completing workflows (donations, leads, etc.).

### Payments Processed
Total number of completed payments in the selected period. This card only appears when Stripe payment settings are configured.

## Topic Actions section

A breakdown of executed topic actions by type for the selected period:

| Action type | What it represents |
|-------------|-------------------|
| **Salesforce Flow** | Flows triggered via the Flow action |
| **Apex Class** | Apex methods called |
| **HTTP Request** | External HTTP endpoints called |
| **Send Email** | Emails sent via topic actions |

Each row includes an inline proportional bar and a count. The total across all types is shown as a badge.

---

## CRM Actions section

Only shown when a CRM integration is connected. Breaks down CRM operations:

| Operation | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Create Record** | New CRM records created |
| **Update Record** | Existing records updated |
| **Query CRM** | Records looked up/searched |

The header shows the name of the connected CRM (e.g., "HubSpot Actions").

---

## Monthly Trend Chart

An interactive bar chart showing activity over time.

**Range toggle:**
- **Last 6 months** — monthly bars for the past 6 months
- **Last 12 months** — monthly bars for the past 12 months

**Metric toggle:**
- **Conversations** — total conversations per month
- **Messages** — total messages per month
- **CRM Actions** — CRM operations per month (when CRM is connected)
- **Topic Actions** — dynamic actions executed per month

Switching metrics or range animates the chart without reloading the page.

---

## Team footer

At the bottom of the dashboard, a footer shows your organization's team member count and your current role.

## Access by role

| Role | Dashboard access |
|------|----------------|
| **Owner** | Full access |
| **Admin** | Full access |
| **Member** | Full access (read-only view) |

All roles can view the analytics dashboard. Members cannot modify any settings from within the dashboard.
